## Ranking Functions

### ROW_NUMBER()

Assigns unique sequential numbers.

### RANK()

Assigns same rank to duplicates and skips the next rank.

Example:
1, 2, 2, 4

### DENSE_RANK()

Assigns same rank to duplicates without skipping ranks.

Example:
1, 2, 2, 3
